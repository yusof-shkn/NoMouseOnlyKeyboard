## 💡 TIPS FOR FASTER ENTRY

1. **Start with Setup Data First** — Complete all areas, stores, suppliers, units, and customers first. This creates the foundation for transactions.

2. **Products Come from NDS** — Do not manually enter products. They are imported from the National Drug Shop (NDS) of Uganda. Product categories are also fixed and managed by the system.

3. **Use Copy-Paste** — Copy the exact text from this document and paste directly into form fields. This prevents typing errors.
